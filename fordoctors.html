Doctor's Chamber on Video Call

Book video call appointments with the country's most qualified specialist doctors, in their private chambers

Here's how

An online chamber works

Create your chamber

Enter your details such as consultation fee and schedule for video consultation hours to create your online chamber.

Your chamber is ready!

After verification and approval, you will have your own chamber link e.g. www.dhakadoctor.live/DrSalahuddin Patients can now directly schedule video-call appointments with you by clicking on your chamber link and paying the consultation fee..

Start Sharing

So next time when someone asks regarding your consultations, simply send them the link or just share it on your facebook..

Digital Promotion

Dhaka Doctor has been built by technology experts. Our digital media professionals will promote your chamber to thousands of patients within hours..

Getting Prepared

Everyday, an hour before your schedule starts, your confirmed appointments for the day will be emailed to you..

Start Consultations

On the time of your schedule, simply open your laptop and click on your chamber link, which will directly start your video call with patients..

Write Prescriptions

Write your prescriptions on your pad and take photos, which will be sent to your patients..

And you’re done!

At the end of your consultation hours, your total consultation fee for the day is transferred to you through bKash...
